~The Cultural Club

The Rubik's Cube Club at MCA (Master of Computer Applications) in MANIT (Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology) is a student-run initiative that focuses on problem-solving skills, promotes teamwork, and organizes events to encourage intellectual growth and innovation among members.
